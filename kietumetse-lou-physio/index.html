About My Practice

With a deep commitment to mental wellness and a person-centered approach, I provide a safe, non-judgmental space where clients can explore their thoughts, feelings, and experiences. My practice is grounded in empathy, respect, and evidence-based therapeutic techniques.

I believe that every individual has the capacity for growth and healing, and my role is to facilitate that process through collaborative, tailored support that honors each person's unique journey and strengths.

My approach integrates various therapeutic modalities to meet each client's specific needs, whether you're dealing with anxiety, depression, relationship challenges, or seeking personal development.

I welcome individuals from all backgrounds and identities, and I'm committed to creating an inclusive, affirming environment where everyone feels seen, heard, and valued.
